INSTRUCTIONS

General

Guess which country the flag belongs to out of the four options presented, you will get 20 randomised flags and the goal is to get as many correct as possible. The flags that have been played in a previous round may be played again in the next.


Flags included in this list are the 193 United Nations member states.


Score history

The score of your first 20 rounds of the game will be displayed in the score history table. It can be reset at any time inbetween rounds.


Buttons

START Starts the game.

RESET HISTORY Resets the history and current scores.
